[h1]GMFU II[/h1]
TFB submod which rebalances, tweaks and fixes stuff for quality gameplay without "cope modding" introduced over the time to the main mod.

[*]UK can annex British malaya after first of january 1938 via decision.

[*]Transfer of states to Manchu after China cap is back
